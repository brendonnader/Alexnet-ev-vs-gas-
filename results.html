Key Takeaways

  • The AlexNet-based model can distinguish between electric and gas vehicles with good accuracy on this dataset.
  • Training and validation curves help confirm that the model is learning without severe overfitting (or reveal if overfitting occurs).
  • Confusion matrix and misclassified examples show where the model struggles, such as cars with similar shapes or unusual angles.
  • With more data, more classes (SUV, sedan, truck, etc.), and further tuning, this approach could support real-world transportation analytics and sustainability monitoring.
