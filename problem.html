The Real-World Problem

As electric vehicles (EVs) become more common, cities, companies, and researchers need better ways to identify and track EVs versus gas-powered cars. Manual checks are slow and inconsistent, especially when there are thousands of vehicles in traffic footage or parking lots.

Automated image-based classification can help support smarter infrastructure planning, emissions analysis, and sustainability reporting.

Why It Matters

  • Sustainability: EV adoption is tied to climate and emissions goals. Knowing how many EVs are on the road helps with policy decisions.
  • Traffic & Infrastructure: Cities can design EV-only zones, charging stations, and parking based on accurate usage data.
  • Business Analytics: Parking garages, toll systems, and fleets may price or manage vehicles differently depending on fuel type.

Dataset Overview

The dataset for this project includes images of electric and gas vehicles. The main structure is: 

train/
  eletric/

  Electric/

All images are resized to 227×227 pixels and normalized so they can be used as input to AlexNet.
